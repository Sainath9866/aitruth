## About Our Judge Model

**TruthMeter-Judge-v1.0** is our proprietary AI model specifically designed for evaluating educational content accuracy.

### Model Specifications
- **Version**: 1.0.0
- **Training Date**: December 2024
- **Training Dataset**: 10,000+ expert-labeled educational Q&A pairs
- **Domains**: Mathematics, Science, History, Literature
- **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Clarity, Completeness

### Technical Details
- Specialized neural architecture optimized for educational content
- Multi-dimensional scoring rubric based on pedagogical research
- Consistent evaluation standards across all subjects
